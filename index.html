saraswathi Vidyalai SCHOOL-CAMPUS

The School follows ICSE syllabus which has true international standards. The syllabus is a balanced education which focuses on all the subjects: arts, languages, maths, science, and sports. ICSE syllabus takes the education from a global perspective, creating a path for a wide variety of opportunities to the children. It is the best syllabus for the children to attempt competitive exams such as NEET, IIT-JEE, SAT, etc.

Digital Classrooms

Children are oriented towards multimedia classes, teaching is given using multimedia aids and most of the classrooms are designed to deliver the lectures digitally. The benefit of multimedia learning is that it takes advantage of the brain’s ability to make connections between verbal and visual representations of content, leading to a deeper understanding, which in turn supports the transfer of learning to other situations. In a multimedia learning environment, students can identify and solve problems more easily compared to the scenario where teaching is made possible only by textbooks. It is always easier if you teach the kids digitally and practically, how something happens rather than explaining the process to them.

Saraswathi Vidyalai as a responsible and dedicated educational society is working towards the development of the child rather than thinking as a business entity. The main goal of Saraswathi Vidyalai is to transform the education system in India from self-centric to the benefits of the society and from marks and bookish knowledge to the intellectuality of the child. Saraswathi Vidyalai is working on bring out the hidden talent of every child and make that ability useful to him in his future endeavors in the best way possible. As an education provider, Saraswathi Vidyalai feels the responsibility in harnessing the curiosity, the creativity, the confidence, the spirituality and the zest for further knowledge, which helps every child to grow beyond what they believe they can be.. Our Mission: To provide the best education system to the children that will be useful in every aspect of their life. The knowledge they gain must be a contribution to society. Our Vision: To inspire a passion for learning and to keep the world within the reach of every child.
